Assignment 0 - September 26, 2016

Getting up and running

Challenges

  • The main challenges of this assignment involved using HTML properly to get a readable site without using CSS.
  • I think this was a good experience for me as it forced me to focus on accurately labelling content, instead of formatting the text to match what I wanted it to look like.

Learning

  • I learned about the <nav> tag, as well as the importance of declaring the 'DOCTYPE' and 'charset'.
  • I was familiar with all of the other HTML elements I used before and the proper hierarchy for them.
  • I was also familiar with developing on flip and pushing frequently to Github.

Resources

  • As always, I heavily turned to w3schools to figure out the proper syntax and usage of tags.
  • I used the Firefox Inspect Element tool to see how websites tended to lay out their articles.
